# Research Sources: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Selection Criteria
The primary focus of this research is strictly on **operators, testers, and builders** rather than general influencers. The chosen experts were selected because they run live experiments, build programmatic systems, and manage real-world AI SEO workflows. We actively filtered out sources that only offer motivational content or general SEO theory without execution data.

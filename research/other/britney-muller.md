## 1. Actionable AI for Marketers (Cohort-based Course)
- **Type:** Educational Course
- **Source:** Maven (Orange Labs Community)
- **Link:** https://maven.com/britney-muller/actionable-ai-for-marketers
- **Summary:**
A practical, hands-on cohort course designed by Britney Muller to help marketers move past the AI hype and build real-world workflow automations. It teaches how to prompt LLM agents for daily research tasks, execute automated SEO/content audits, evaluate model biases, and implement strict human-in-the-loop guardrails.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **No-Code Automation:** Marketers can build complex agentic workflows (e.g. automated competitor audits or job boards) using plain English instructions rather than coding.
    - **Ethical Guardrails:** Emphasizes that AI models are merely "spicy autocomplete" systems that lack real-world awareness, requiring strict human editing and oversight.
    - **Practical AI Literacy:** Focuses on understanding LLM training data and grounding mechanisms to build safe and sustainable marketing operations.

## 2. Google AI Mode: What Matters for SEO & Marketing
- **Type:** Expert Industry Analysis
- **Source:** Search Engine Land
- **Link:** https://searchengineland.com/google-ai-mode-what-matters-seo-marketing-453461
- **Summary:**
Britney Muller analyzes how Google's transition to AI Mode shifts the search landscape. Since users receive synthesized answers directly on search engine results pages (SERPs), traditional SEO metrics like keyword rankings and click-through rates (CTR) are declining. Marketers must pivot from pure search engine optimization to optimizing for brand citations and authority.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **Mentions over Links:** In LLM grounding systems, contextual brand mentions and web-wide discussions act as the new authority signals, replacing standard backlink metrics.
    - **Optimizing the RAG Layer:** You cannot hack an LLM model, but you can optimize the RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) layer by writing highly structured, factual, and easily extractable content.
    - **Query Fan-out Strategy:** Google AI Mode breaks down prompts into multiple related sub-queries. Content must be comprehensive and structured to satisfy these hidden intermediate intents.
    - **New Citation KPIs:** Success should be measured by AI citation rates, presence in generative summaries, and overall share of voice in LLM answers.
- **Why it matters:** It provides a clear blueprint for adapting to the zero-click SERP era, proving that building brand reputation and writing highly structured, "quotable" content is the only way to remain visible.

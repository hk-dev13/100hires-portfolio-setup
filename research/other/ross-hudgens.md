## 1. Versus Pages: The Best Transactional Content Template for AI Search Traffic
- **Type:** Statistical Content Study
- **Source:** Siege Media Research
- **Link:** https://www.siegemedia.com/research/versus-pages
- **Summary:**
A study of 116 B2B GA4 websites measuring the correlation between B2B comparison page libraries and AI search referrals (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, etc.). The study reveals that direct "versus" (X vs. Y) pages are the single strongest content driver of AI traffic, out-correlating "alternatives" and "best software" listicles by a wide margin.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **Versus (VS) Page Superiority:** Versus pages have a Spearman rank correlation of 0.65 with AI search traffic, double that of alternative lists. This is because LLMs citation graphs directly match the exact "X vs. Y" query structure.
    - **Volume Scale Impact:** Median AI search sessions grow 350% when moving from 1-5 comparison pages to a library of 6-20 pages. Sites with 21+ comparison pages see a 900% median lift.
    - **Reduced Affiliate Competition:** Listicles and alternative roundups compete directly with high-authority directory sites (G2, Capterra) for citation slots, whereas versus pages face less competition.
    - **The 28% Leverage Rule:** Content volume explains approximately 28% of the variance in AI search referrals. The remaining 72% is dictated by off-page brand authority, category fit, and overall citation density.

## 2. Why AI Overviews Are Actually Increasing Your Homepage Traffic
- **Type:** Traffic & Search Analytics Case Study
- **Source:** Siege Media Strategy
- **Link:** https://www.siegemedia.com/strategy/ai-homepage-traffic-increase
- **Summary:**
An analysis of Search Console and GA4 data for 50 national websites (28 B2B, 22 B2C) investigating traffic shifts after the accelerated rollout of AI Overviews and LLMs. The data indicates that while overall sitewide informational traffic is down, homepage clicks and impressions are surging as users perform branded queries after seeing brands cited in AI answers.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **Branded Search Halo Effect:** LLMs and AIOs act as mid-funnel brand validators. Users see a brand referenced in an overview and then search the brand name directly, leading to an increase in homepage clicks (e.g. Siege Media's homepage clicks rose 29.6%).
    - **Conversion Multiplier:** Homepage traffic converts at a much higher rate (3-8%) than top-of-funnel informational blog posts (1-4%). As a result, net conversions can stay positive even during traffic declines.
    - **The Brand Dividend:** This homepage traffic shift only benefits real brands with established authority. Anonymous, unbranded informational sites do not capture this branded search loop and suffer major traffic losses.
    - **Optimization Recommendations:** Focus on optimizing homepage conversion rates, bid to defend branded keywords, retarget homepage visitors, and prioritize brand building.

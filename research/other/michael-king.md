# Other Research Materials

**Expert:** Michael King

## 1. The AI Search Manual
- **Type:** Comprehensive Guide / Methodology
- **Source:** iPullRank
- **Link:** https://ipullrank.com/ai-search-manual
- **Summary:** 
"The AI Search Manual" is a definitive operational guide created by Michael King and the iPullRank team to navigate the shift from traditional SEO to Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). It outlines the methodology of "Relevance Engineering" to maximize brand visibility across AI-driven search surfaces like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and AI Overviews.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **Relevance Engineering:** A multi-disciplinary approach combining information retrieval, content strategy, UX, and digital PR.
    - **AI Mechanics:** Marketers must understand underlying systems like semantic relevance, query fan-out, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) rather than relying on legacy keyword checklists.
- **Why it matters:** It provides an actionable, evidence-based framework for brands to remain visible as user journeys shift toward conversational AI.

## 2. Search Telemetry Project & Agentic RAG
- **Type:** Open-Source Tooling / Technical Case Study
- **Source:** iPullRank Blog & Search Engine Land
- **Link:** https://ipullrank.com/probability-ai-search
- **Summary:**
Under Michael King's leadership, iPullRank developed the Search Telemetry Project to derive thousands of features per URL to approximate internal search quality signals. Furthermore, King has been exploring "Agentic RAG" audit harnesses to observe how complex, multi-step AI agents (using planners, routers, retrievers, and critics) evaluate and synthesize web content.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **Agentic RAG:** Traditional RAG is evolving into multi-agent systems that reason over content.
    - **Data-Driven Quality:** Content evaluation can be systematically reverse-engineered by deriving features that mimic machine-learning quality signals.
- **Why it matters:** By open-sourcing these tools, King provides the SEO community with the means to empirically test how AI search engines interpret their pages.

## 3. The Science of SEO
- **Type:** Book (Forthcoming)
- **Source:** Various Book Retailers (Wiley)
- **Link:** https://ipullrank.com/seo-week-day-one-science
- **Summary:**
A highly anticipated book authored by Michael King that dives deeply into the foundational computer science, machine learning, and information retrieval methodologies behind search engine algorithms. It serves as the technical, engineering-focused companion to standard marketing-oriented SEO books.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **Engineering First:** SEO practitioners must bridge the gap between marketing tactics and underlying computer science to remain effective in the AI era.
    - **Information Retrieval:** A deep understanding of IR and Natural Language Processing (NLP) is now mandatory for modern SEO success.
- **Why it matters:** It validates the argument that SEO is fundamentally a technical discipline, laying the groundwork for AI SEO strategies.

## 1. The Fan-out Framework
- **Type:** Methodology / SEO Strategy
- **Source:** Zyppy Signal
- **Link:** https://signal.zyppy.com/p/fan-out-framework
- **Summary:**
A comprehensive guide written by Cyrus Shepard outlining the "Fan-out Framework," a structured 5-step approach to capture traffic from AI search engines. It explains that AI tools split a user's prompt into multiple hidden "fan-out queries" (sub-queries). To optimize AI search presence, websites must target this entire cluster of intents rather than single keywords.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **Identify Core Keyword:** Begin with keyword topics your website already ranks for.
    - **Find Fan-out Queries:** Research the associated questions and sub-queries the LLM spawns.
    - **Prioritize Topics:** Determine which related sub-topics carry the most value and user intent.
    - **Consolidate existing content:** Update high-ranking existing URLs to fill intent gaps rather than creating thin, new AI-generated pages.
    - **Pitfalls to Avoid:** Chasing every single synthetic query is a trap. Consolidating into topic "neighborhoods" is much more effective.

## 2. AI Citation Ranking Factors & 7-Step Audit Checklist
- **Type:** Meta-Analysis / Case Study
- **Source:** Zyppy Signal
- **Link:** https://signal.zyppy.com/p/ai-citation-ranking-factors
- **Summary:**
Cyrus Shepard compiled a meta-analysis of 54 different experiments, patents, and studies on AI search citations. The research identifies the key variables that influence whether a URL is cited by AI engines like Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and ChatGPT. He translates these findings into an actionable 7-step AI citation audit.
- **Key Takeaways:**
    - **The 4 Pillars:** AI citation success is heavily anchored to Relevance, Trust, Topical Authority, and Extractability.
    - **The 7-Step Checklist:**
        1. **Accessibility:** Ensure AI bots are not blocked by robots.txt or Cloudflare settings.
        2. **Main Query Ranking:** Target search terms where your site already ranks in traditional top organic positions.
        3. **Related Queries:** Target and rank for related fan-out queries.
        4. **Content Format:** Select the appropriate layout (e.g., tables, Q&A blocks).
        5. **Direct Matching:** Ensure content explicitly phrases answers to match search intent.
        6. **Positioning:** Place critical answers near the top of the page.
        7. **Citation Readiness:** Structure data so AI crawlers can easily reference your content.
- **Why it matters:** It validates the fact that GEO is built directly on traditional technical SEO and content accessibility, stripping away speculative "hacks" in favor of solid ranking fundamentals.

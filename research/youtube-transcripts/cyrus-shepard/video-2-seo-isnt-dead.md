## Executive Summary

Cyrus Shepard joins host Vince Nero to discuss how search is shifting, focusing on how Google is layering new user engagement and brand signals on top of traditional rankings. They cover the decline of passive affiliate websites, the importance of building a recognizable brand, the shift in anchor text profiles (winners having more branded anchor text), and the necessity of creating original, evidence-based content.

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Layering Signals:** Traditional SEO signals (links, anchor text, content) still matter, but Google is layering new user satisfaction and brand authority signals on top of them.
- **High Bar for Affiliate Sites:** Traditional thin affiliate sites are declining. To survive, publishers must conduct actual product testing, tear-downs, and proprietary data gathering (e.g., RunRepeat mattress tests).
- **Branded Link Profiles:** Winning sites in recent Google updates typically have a high percentage of branded anchor text (around 20%), demonstrating organic brand trust.
- **PR-driven Link Building:** SEO link building should focus on brand story and executive PR rather than standalone link-bait studies, helping to build a defensible business moat.

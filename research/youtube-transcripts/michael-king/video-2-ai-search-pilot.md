## Executive Summary

In this short walkthrough, Michael King details iPullRank's "AI Search Pilot" program, explaining why traditional SEO is no longer sufficient. He outlines a comprehensive strategy that includes technical AI search audits, keyword matrices for synthetic queries, and omnimedia content planning, emphasizing that AI search optimization requires a broader ecosystem approach rather than just website tweaks.

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Beyond Traditional SEO:** Treating AI search optimization as "just SEO" will fail; it requires specific technical audits geared toward LLM visibility.
- **Synthetic Queries:** AI platforms extrapolate user prompts into series of "synthetic queries" in the background, which must be identified and mapped to seed queries.
- **Omnimedia is Critical:** Optimization must extend beyond owned websites to include social media, earned media, and especially YouTube (the second most cited source in AI search).
- **New Measurement Paradigms:** Because AI search changes user behavior, brands must build new data pipelines and measurement dashboards to accurately track success.
